About

Shaping cities
for people

Noal Leonetti is a graduate student pursuing a Master of Urban Planning at the University of Washington's College of Built Environments in Seattle. He is driven by a deep belief that thoughtful, evidence-based planning can create cities that are more livable, resilient, and equitable for everyone.

With a commitment to sustainability and environmental stewardship, Noal approaches urban challenges by bridging the gap between policy, community needs, and the built environment — always with people at the center.

Whether through GIS mapping, community engagement, or policy research, he brings a collaborative, rigorous, and human-centered approach to every project he takes on.
